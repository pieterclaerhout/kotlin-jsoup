Help

What is a Foto-Flat

At Sportograf a Foto-Flat is a bundle of photos for a fixed price. It includes *all* personal photos of one athlete (start number) and a selection of impression photos we took at your event. The number of pictures may vary. We do not always find all photos automatically. Further photos showing you can be added for free!

Please note: Our Foto-Flat does not include photos of your family- or groupmembers, best mates, partners, etc. It is only for one person!

(*Spartans, every distance requires a new flatrate! Arroooo!)

Can I buy single photos?

For most events you can buy single photos. Click on the photo to see the price.
Of course, there are exceptions to the rule. For example, on multi-day stage races, we offer only the entire package.
Please search for your event and get the options and prices.

Do you have more photos of me?

Maybe!

Sometimes it isn't possible for us to recognize you on our photos and to add the photos automatically to your start number.
But we have some clever options you can use to find more photos of you and add them to your start number easily.

How does it work?

1. Find additional photos of you

2. Tag the photos with your start number and leave your email address

3. We check it, and notify you

Can I buy my Foto-Flat now and look for additional photos later?

YES YOU CAN! As soon as you have tagged a photo with your start number and we have checked it, you will receive an email from us and the photo will automatically be added to your order.

By the way, it also works the other way round. You can mark photos with your start number, have them checked by us and place your order afterwards.

How can I add photos to my start number?

You have found photos showing you which are not included in your Foto-Flat yet?
Congrats! You did it right! Please follow the 3 steps to add further photos to your Foto-Flat:

1. Find additional photos of you

2. Tag the photos with your start number and leave your email address

3. We check it, and notify you
